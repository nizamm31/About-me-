My School Story

KINDERGARTEN

I went to kindergarten near my house, I used to live in Jakarta then I returned to Yogyakarta to study, there are not many stories about kindergarten. But I am happy to be able to go to school there.

ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

I attended elementary school at Mi Husnayain, a private school, where I studied for 6 years, and was often active in participating in competitions, such as chess and scouts, I also joined the scout organization.

JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL

I went to junior high school at MTS Sunan Pandanaran, I studied there for 3 years and I was also active, such as joining HTT and magazine organizations, I also won a choir competition when I was in junior high school.

SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

During my high school years, I was still in the process of finishing school, I attended the Al-Quran science high school.
